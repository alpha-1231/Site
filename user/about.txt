OVERVIEW
--------

AboutMySchool is Nepal's dedicated online educational directory, built to
bridge the information gap between students, parents, and educational
institutions across the country. Whether you are a student looking for the
right college, a parent researching school options for your child, or an
educator exploring affiliated institutions, AboutMySchool is the single
platform where every educational institution in Nepal can be discovered,
explored, and evaluated — all in one place.

The platform serves as a free, publicly accessible resource that aggregates
detailed, accurate, and up-to-date profiles of schools, colleges, universities,
training institutes, and other educational bodies operating throughout Nepal.
From the hills of Pokhara to the plains of the Terai, and from the busy streets
of Kathmandu to remote districts, AboutMySchool aims to put every institution
on the digital map.


WHAT IS ABOUTMYSCHOOL?
-----------------------

AboutMySchool is not just a list of schools. It is a fully detailed institutional
directory designed with depth and usability in mind. Each institution listed on
the platform comes with a rich, informative profile that goes far beyond a simple
name and address. Users get a 360-degree view of each institution through
multimedia content, structured data, and direct digital connections.

The site exists because finding reliable, complete, and visually rich information
about educational institutions in Nepal has historically been difficult. Parents
spend hours calling schools or travelling in person just to gather basic details.
Students struggle to compare institutions when applying. AboutMySchool solves
this problem by putting all essential information — and more — in a single,
searchable, filterable, and visually rich directory.


INSTITUTION PROFILES — WHAT YOU WILL FIND
-------------------------------------------

Every institution listed on AboutMySchool has its own dedicated profile page
packed with information across multiple categories:

1. GENERAL INFORMATION
   - Full official name of the institution
   - Type of institution (school, college, university, technical institute, etc.)
   - Level of education offered (primary, lower secondary, secondary, higher
     secondary, bachelor, master, doctoral, vocational/technical)
   - Year of establishment
   - Affiliation details (which university, board, or body the institution is
     affiliated with — such as Tribhuvan University, Pokhara University,
     Kathmandu University, CTEVT, NEB, CDC, and others)
   - Registration and recognition details

2. LOCATION & ADDRESS
   - Province, district, and municipality
   - Ward number and tole/locality
   - Full postal address
   - Embedded map or geographic reference for easy navigation

3. CONTACT & SOCIAL PRESENCE
   - Phone numbers (office, principal, admission)
   - Email address
   - Official website link
   - Social media links including Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, Twitter/X,
     LinkedIn, and TikTok where available
   - Direct messaging or inquiry options

4. MULTIMEDIA CONTENT
   - Photo gallery: official institution photographs, campus images, classrooms,
     labs, libraries, sports facilities, events, and more
   - Audio: institution introduction audio clips, welcome messages, or
     audio descriptions where provided
   - Video: promotional videos, campus tour videos, admission guidance videos,
     event highlights, and YouTube channel integrations — giving prospective
     students and parents a real feel of the institution before visiting

5. ACADEMIC & ADMINISTRATIVE DETAILS
   - Faculties and departments offered
   - Programs and courses available
   - Admission requirements and process
   - Fee structure (where disclosed)
   - Scholarships and financial aid information
   - Medium of instruction (Nepali, English, or bilingual)
   - Total student capacity and current enrolment (where available)
   - Teacher-to-student ratio
   - Available infrastructure (science labs, computer labs, library, hostel,
     sports grounds, canteen, transport, etc.)


THE SEARCH SYSTEM
------------------

At the heart of AboutMySchool is a powerful search system that allows users to
instantly find institutions matching their specific needs. The search bar is
prominently placed on the homepage and is accessible from every page of the
site. Users can type any keyword — the institution's name, a subject or program,
or a place name — and receive instant, relevant results from the directory.

The search is designed to be fast, forgiving of spelling variations, and useful
even with partial information. Whether a user types "Kathmandu engineering college"
or just "engineering Kathmandu," the system surfaces the most relevant matches.


FILTER SYSTEM — NARROW DOWN YOUR SEARCH
-----------------------------------------

To make discovery even easier, AboutMySchool provides a multi-dimensional filter
system that lets users slice and narrow the full institution list by several key
criteria simultaneously. The filters are:

1. ADDRESS / LOCATION FILTER
   Users can filter institutions by geographic area — from the province level down
   to the district and municipality. This makes it easy to find all institutions
   in a specific area, whether a user is looking for schools in Lalitpur, colleges
   in Butwal, or universities in Pokhara. The location filter is especially useful
   for families looking for institutions near their home or within a preferred
   region of Nepal.

2. TYPE FILTER
   Institutions on AboutMySchool span a wide range — from basic primary schools to
   vocational training centres to full universities. The type filter lets users
   select exactly the kind of institution they are interested in:
   - School (primary, lower secondary, secondary)
   - Higher Secondary School (plus two / +2)
   - College
   - University / Campus
   - Technical and Vocational Institute
   - Medical and Health Sciences Institution
   - Law College
   - Management Institute
   - Engineering College
   - Other specialised institutions

3. LEVEL FILTER
   This filter targets the level of education the institution provides:
   - Early Childhood Development (ECD) / Pre-Primary
   - Primary (Grade 1–5)
   - Lower Secondary (Grade 6–8)
   - Secondary (Grade 9–10)
   - Higher Secondary / +2 (Grade 11–12)
   - Bachelor Level
   - Master Level
   - M.Phil / Doctoral Level
   - Certificate and Diploma Level
   - Short-term Vocational / Skill Training

4. AFFILIATION FILTER
   Affiliation is a critical factor for students choosing a college or university.
   This filter allows users to sort institutions by their governing affiliating
   body, including but not limited to:
   - Tribhuvan University (TU)
   - Kathmandu University (KU)
   - Pokhara University (PU)
   - Purbanchal University
   - Mid-Western University
   - Far-Western University
   - Lumbini Buddhist University
   - Agriculture and Forestry University (AFU)
   - Nepal Open University
   - Council for Technical Education and Vocational Training (CTEVT)
   - National Examinations Board (NEB)
   - Curriculum Development Centre (CDC)
   - Private Boards and International Affiliations

Filters can be combined freely. A user can, for example, filter for all
bachelor-level colleges affiliated with Tribhuvan University located in
Bharatpur, Chitwan — and receive a focused, relevant list instantly.

WHY ABOUTMYSCHOOL MATTERS FOR NEPAL
-------------------------------------

Nepal's educational sector is large, diverse, and geographically spread across
challenging terrain. There are thousands of registered educational institutions
spread across 77 districts and 7 provinces. Historically, information about most
of these institutions has been scattered — on outdated government registers, word
of mouth, or fragmented social media pages.

AboutMySchool changes this by:

- Creating a centralised, modern, and user-friendly home for all educational
  institution data in Nepal
- Making information accessible to anyone with an internet connection, reducing
  geographic and economic barriers to informed decision-making
- Supporting Nepal's growing internet-savvy population who expect to research
  important decisions online before acting
- Helping smaller institutions in less-prominent districts get the same digital
  visibility as well-established Kathmandu institutions
- Supporting parents and students in rural Nepal who cannot easily travel to
  visit multiple institutions before choosing


MEDIA-RICH EXPERIENCE
----------------------

What sets AboutMySchool apart from a plain directory or a government list is
its commitment to a rich media experience. Text alone cannot convey the feel
of a campus, the energy of a school's community, or the quality of its
facilities. That is why each institution profile on AboutMySchool is designed
to carry:

IMAGES
Professional and candid photographs covering campus buildings, classrooms,
laboratories, sports fields, cultural events, graduation ceremonies, and
student life. Images are displayed in a clean, organised gallery format that
users can browse at their own pace.

AUDIO
Some institutions provide audio introductions — a welcome message from the
principal, an overview of programs, or a brief audio tour. This is particularly
valuable for users with limited data access or those who prefer audio-first
browsing.

VIDEO
Video is the most powerful medium for institutional marketing and transparency.
AboutMySchool supports embedded video content including YouTube channel videos,
campus tour recordings, student testimonials, faculty introductions, and event
recaps. Video brings an institution to life in a way that text and photos alone
cannot.

आफ्नो शैक्षिक लक्ष्य, मनपर्ने स्थान र बजेटसँग संस्थाहरू मिलाउन मद्दत गर्दछ।

गर्छन्। AboutMySchool ले अभिभावकहरूलाई सम्पूर्ण तस्वीर दिन्छ — संस्थामा कल

ले युट्युब च्यानल भिडियोहरू, क्याम्पस भ्रमण रेकर्डिङहरू, विद्यार्थी प्रशंसापत्रहरू,

समावेशिता
प्लेटफर्म सम्पूर्ण नेपालका लागि निर्मित छ — केवल काठमाडौंका लागि मात्र होइन।
सुदूर जिल्ला र कम सेवा प्राप्त प्रदेशका संस्थाहरूले प्रमुख सहरी केन्द्रका
संस्थाहरूजस्तै गुणस्तरको प्रोफाइल र दृश्यता पाउँछन्।

पहुँचयोग्यता
साइट कम-ब्यान्डविड्थ जडान र मोबाइल उपकरणहरूमा पहुँचयोग्य हुने गरी डिजाइन
गरिएको छ — यो स्वीकार गर्दै कि नेपालको जनसंख्याको महत्वपूर्ण हिस्साले
मुख्यतया स्मार्टफोनमार्फत इन्टरनेट पहुँच गर्दछ।

पारदर्शिता
संस्थागत डेटा सार्वजनिक रूपमा उपलब्ध गराएर, AboutMySchool ले शिक्षा क्षेत्रमा
पारदर्शितालाई बढावा दिन्छ, प्रयोगकर्ताहरूलाई सूचित निर्णय लिन मद्दत गर्दछ
र संस्थाहरूलाई उनीहरूले प्रस्तुत गर्ने जानकारीप्रति जवाफदेही राख्दछ।

--------------------

AboutMySchool को दृष्टिकोण नेपालमा शिक्षाको लागि निश्चित डिजिटल सन्दर्भ बन्नु
हो — पहिलो ठाउँ जहाँ कुनै पनि विद्यार्थी, अभिभावक वा शिक्षक देशको कुनै पनि
कुनामा रहेको कुनै पनि शैक्षिक संस्थाबारे जानकारी चाहँदा जान्छन्। प्लेटफर्मले
नेपालको द्रुत रूपमा विस्तार हुँदै गरेको शिक्षा क्षेत्रसँगै वृद्धि गर्ने,
निरन्तर नयाँ संस्थाहरू थप्ने, डेटा गुणस्तर सुधार गर्ने र नेपालको शैक्षिक
समुदायको विकसित आवश्यकताहरू पूरा गर्ने नयाँ सुविधाहरू पेश गर्ने लक्ष्य राख्दछ।

जसले उनीहरूलाई शिक्षाबारे आत्मविश्वासपूर्ण, सूचित निर्णय लिन सशक्त बनाओस्।


सम्पर्क र सूचीकरण
--------------------

प्रोत्साहन गरिन्छ। सूचीकरण निःशुल्क छ र प्रक्रिया सरल र सहज बनाइएको छ।
